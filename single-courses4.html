Course Syllabus

Effective communication

Lessons

  • Introduction
  • 1. Introduction to Public Speaking The benefits of public speaking, communication processes, ethical speaking and categories of speeches
  • 2. Analyzing Your Audience Adapting to audiences, evaluation techniques and listener needs
  • 3. Listening and Feedback Improving listening and note-taking skills, the four stages of listening and the different types of listening
  • 4. Selecting the Topic, Purpose and Thesis of Your Speech General and specific purpose speeches, developing a thesis and timed speeches
  • 5. Researching the Speech Research strategies and types, source reliability and supporting materials
  • 6. Organizing the Speech Organizational patterns for informative and persuasive speeches, main ideas and supporting ideas
  • 7. Outlining the Speech Introduction, body and conclusion of a speech
  • 8. Language and Style Inclusive and vivid language, diction and speaking styless
  • 9. Speech Delivery Four categories of speech delivery, nonverbal communication, pronunciation and speech rehearsal
  • 10. Selecting and Incorporating Visual Supports Advantages and disadvantages of PowerPoint, copyright and fair use issues and preparing visual aids
  • 11. Types of Speeches Informative, persuasive and special occasion speeches
  • 12. Reasoning and Rhetorical Proof Significance of ethos, logos and pathos; persuasive speech and types of reasoning
