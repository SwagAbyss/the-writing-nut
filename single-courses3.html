Course Syllabus

Effective communication

Lessons

  • Introduction
  • 1. Idioms: Introduction to idiomatic expressions and their significance in communication.
  • 2. Phrases and Phrasal Verbs: Understanding phrases and their role in enriching language. Exploring phrasal verbs and their usage in different contexts.
  • 3. Slangs (American and British): Unraveling the charm of informal language through American and British slangs.
  • 4. Tamil Equivalents: Discovering Tamil equivalents for common English expressions. Analyzing language nuances and cultural implications.
  • 5. Euphemistic Expressions: Understanding the art of euphemism to communicate sensitively.
  • 6. Lexicon Building: Expanding your vocabulary through systematic lexicon building techniques.

What you'll learn

  • Colorful expressions that add flair to your language
  • Master essential phrases and their versatile usage.
  • Embrace the language's modern edge with popular slangs.
  • Discover regional equivalents to enhance your bilingual proficiency.
  • Expand your vocabulary horizons for confident communication
